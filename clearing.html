Clearing :

Shipping internationally can be a complex process, especially when it comes to customs clearance. At ePort Logistics, we take the stress out of the equation with our comprehensive Clearance as a Service.

Here's how we ensure your cargo moves seamlessly through customs:

  • Convenient Carting Space Management: We understand the importance of efficient space utilization. Our team expertly manages carting space allocation, ensuring your cargo is cleared promptly and avoids unnecessary delays due to congestion.
  • Thorough Documentation Support: Documentation errors are a leading cause of customs hold-ups. Our experts meticulously review and prepare all necessary paperwork, including invoices, certificates of origin, packing lists, and any other required documents, to meet stringent customs regulations.
  • Efficient Consolidation Services: For smaller shipments, we offer cost-effective consolidation services. We combine your cargo with others headed for the same destination, streamlining the clearance process and reducing your overall costs.
  • Certification Solutions: Many goods require specific certifications for import or export. We can guide you on the necessary certifications and assist in obtaining them to ensure a smooth customs clearance process.

Benefits of ePort Logistics' Clearance as a Service:

  • Reduced Risk of Delays: Our meticulous documentation and expertise minimize the risk of customs hold-ups, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination on time.
  • Improved Efficiency: Streamlined carting space management and consolidation services contribute to a faster and more efficient clearance process.
  • Cost Savings: We help you avoid penalties and unnecessary charges associated with incorrect documentation or delays.
  • Peace of Mind: Our team of clearance specialists handles all the complexities, allowing you to focus on your core business activities.
