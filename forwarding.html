Sea Freight:

ePort Logistics offers a robust suite of sea freight services to meet the demands of any shipment size:

  • Sea Freight Options: We provide flexibility with Groupage (consolidated shipments), Full Container Loads (FCL), and Less Than Container Loads (LCL).
  • Container Dispatch: Choose from a variety of container sizes, including 20', 40', and 40' high cube, to perfectly accommodate your cargo. We can also arrange for specialized equipment like flat racks and Ro/Ro vessels for oversized or project cargo.
  • Expert Handling: Our team can handle all aspects of loading and securing your cargo, including stuffing, lashing, and on-site support.
  • Diverse Cargo Expertise: We have extensive experience transporting a wide range of goods, including heavy cargo, bulk cargo, project cargo, individually packaged items, hazardous materials, and perishables.
  • Sea Import and Export: Whether you're importing or exporting goods by sea, ePort Logistics is your one-stop solution for a smooth and efficient process.
  • Multi-Modal Options: We can seamlessly integrate sea freight with air or surface transportation to create a customized multi-modal solution that best suits your requirements.
