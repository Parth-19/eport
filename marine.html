Safeguard Your Shipment with ePort Logistics' Comprehensive Insurance Solutions
The world of international trade is full of potential risks, from physical damage to non-payment by buyers. At ePort Logistics, we go beyond efficient transportation by offering a comprehensive suite of insurance solutions to protect your cargo and your bottom line. Don't leave your business exposed to unexpected losses!

Tailored Coverage:
We offer a variety of insurance options to fit your specific needs. Whether you need basic protection against physical damage during transit or comprehensive coverage for non-payment and political risks, we can create a customized plan.

Marine Cargo Insurance:
This essential insurance protects your cargo against physical loss or damage during transportation by sea, air, or inland waterways. Coverage typically includes perils like fire, theft, collision, and weather events.

Cargo Liability Insurance:
This insurance protects you from legal liability for third-party claims related to your cargo, such as damage caused by defective packaging or spills.

Inland Marine Insurance:
This broader coverage extends beyond traditional marine insurance, offering protection for cargo during inland transportation by truck or rail.

Trade Credit Insurance (ECI/ECGC):
This insurance safeguards your business against non-payment by the buyer due to commercial or political risks, such as insolvency, war, or currency inconvertibility. ECI/ECGC options can be integrated with marine cargo insurance for a truly comprehensive safety net.

  • Financial Protection: Insurance minimizes the financial impact of potential risks, ensuring your business remains profitable.
  • Flexibility: We offer a range of insurance options to tailor coverage to your specific needs and budget.
  • Expert Guidance: Our team of insurance specialists will guide you through the options and help you choose the most suitable coverage for your cargo and trade route.
